Video Production

In addition to live-event videography, Media Services also provides limited video production for WCC staff, the WCC Foundation, and the Board of Trustees. Examples of this type of production would be Staff Orientation or Departmental Training videos.

While we do not develop instructional content for faculty, our department is equipped with a post-production video editing suite that staff and faculty can use to create their own content.

If you have questions about our videography services please contact Warren Nelson at 734-477-8558.

To reserve the post-production video suite, please contact Becca Meixner at 734-973-3383.

Video Production Questions

For more information regarding our videography services or if you require training in our post-production suite, please contact Warren Nelson at 734-477-8558.
