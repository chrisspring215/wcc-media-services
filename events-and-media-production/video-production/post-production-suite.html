Post-Production Editing Suite

Media Serves provides a post-production video and audio editing suite for staff and faculty to create their own education and informational media content. The suite is located on the 2nd floor of the GM building in room GM-223H.

To reserve a time slot or if you have questions regarding scheduling time please call Becca Meixner at 734-973-3383.

While we do not offer in-depth training on the equipment and software for the post-production editing suite, if you would like someone to show you the basic functionality of the system and programs please contact Warren Nelson at 734-477-8558.
