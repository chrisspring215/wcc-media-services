Seminar Rooms

The Morris Lawrence Building is home to several seminar rooms that can be used for one-off meetings or as break-out rooms for a larger events.

The largest room (ML-100), has audiovisual capabilities similar to WCC's TEC Rooms. The other rooms can be equipped with mobile audio and video equipment for multimedia presentations

Booking and pricing for all of WCC's live event venues is conducted through the Department of Conference Services. If you are interested in booking an event, please contact Patrick Downey (email) at 734-677-5033.

Visit our Live Event Venues page to view WCC's other live event venues.
