Event Venues

WCC is equipped with several live-event venue locations across its campus.

Our flagship venue, Towsley Auditorium, is a 495-seat, fully equipped venue ideal for live-performances and large conferences located in the Morris Lawrence Building.

The Morris Lawrence building also contains several Seminar Rooms suited for hosting breakout sessions and medium-sized conferences.

Smaller productions can also be hosted in our College Theater located in the LA building.
