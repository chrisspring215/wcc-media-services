Event and Media Production

Media Services provides live-event services and videography services for staff, faculty and community partners. The department also provides a fully-functional post-production video suite where staff and faculty can edit video content.

Our Live-Event production team can help ensure that your seminar or production is a success. Let us work with you to ensure your event is professionally produced and problem-free.

WCC is home to several theaters and large seminar rooms. Our flagship venue, Towsley Auditorium in the Morris Lawrence Building, is equipped with sophisticated audio, lighting, projection and video systems that can enhance any conference, performance or lecture.
