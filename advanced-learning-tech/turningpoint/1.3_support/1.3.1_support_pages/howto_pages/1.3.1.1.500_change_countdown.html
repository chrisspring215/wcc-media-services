Changing a Countdown's duration:

Method #1:

  1. Click on the Countdown graphic
  2. In the “TurningPoint” window, enter a value other than “10”

Method #2

  1. From the “TurningPoint 2008” tab, click on the “Tools” icon.
  2. Scroll down to “Settings” and select it.
  3. In “Settings” on the left-hand side of the screen, make sure that the slide that you want to alter is selected. Or, select “Presentation” to alter all slides in the presentation.
  4. Scroll down to the “Response Reminders Settings” section.
  5. Set “Countdown Seconds” to a value other than 10.
  6. Click “Done” button in the bottom right hand corner.
  7. Remember that during a presentation Polling will close automatically when the Countdown is complete.
