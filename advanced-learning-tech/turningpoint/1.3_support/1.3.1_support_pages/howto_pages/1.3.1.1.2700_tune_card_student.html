Set the Turning Point Clicker to the proper channel

  1. Push the “GO” or "CH" button on TurningPoint clicker. The LED on the card will begin flashing red and green.
  2. Enter the two digit RF channel you would like to change the clickers to send. Example: if you are trying to change the clickers to channel “41”, enter “4” then “1”.
  3. The LED light on the TurningPoint clicker should turn Solid Amber.
  4. Push “GO” button again on TurningPoint clicker.
  5. The LED light on the TurningPoint clicker should turn Solid Green.
  6. The TurningPoint clicker is now set. `
