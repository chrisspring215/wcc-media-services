Adding a Countdown:

  1. From the “TurningPoint 2008” tab, click on the “Insert Object” icon.
  2. Scroll down to “Countdown” and select it.
  3. TurningPoint will automatically insert a countdown animation in the lower right hand corner of the slide. (While previous versions of TurningPoint allowed you to select from several types of countdown animations, the current version has limited the user to only one standard countdown animation.)

    NOTE: By default, a Countdown will not begin automatically - you must advance the slide to make the Countdown begin. If you are trying to add a Countdown that begins automatically, click here.

    NOTE: During a presentation polling will close automatically when the Countdown is complete.
