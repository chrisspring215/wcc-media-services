Non-Response Grids in TurningPoint

A Non-Response Grid can be added to any TurningPoint slide to give responders and presenters a visual conformation that a student’s clicker has been recorded. While a Non-Response Grid looks similar to a Response Grid, their functionality differs. When using a Non-Response Grid, a response from a clicker will remove that clicker’s corresponding box within the grid.

Adding a Non-Response Grid

  1. From the “TurningPoint 2008” tab, click on the “Insert Object” icon.
  2. Scroll down to “Grid” and select “Non-Response Grid." `

    When running a presentation, a TurningPoint slide that contains a non-response grid will display a series of boxes in the lower section of the screen. If you are using a Participant List with the names of students included, their names will be displayed in the box. As students click in, their corresponding box will be removed from the grid.
