Creating countdowns that begin automatically:

  1. Choose the TurningPoint slide that has the “Countdown” applied.
  2. From the “Animation” pane, click on the “Custom Animation” icon. The “Custom Animation” column will appear at the side of the screen.
  3. Select "Countdown New" from the list of animations. You will see the "Modify: Appear" settings become active
  4. From the "Start:" drop down menu select "With Previous".

    NOTE: Remember that during a presentation Polling will close automatically when the Countdown is complete.
