How to get a TurningPoint System
Home >  How to get a TurningPoint System

WCC's TurningPoint Initiative is open to all WCC staff and Faculty, both full and part-time.

**If you are a student and are looking for student related TurningPoint information Click Here.**

As of the Winter 2012 Semester, WCC has shifted to a student-ownership model for TurningPoint clickers. The new model simplifies the process for acquiring TurningPoint hardware and creating student Participant Lists. The major changes to the TurningPoint Program are:

  • Students now purchase their own clickers from the WCC bookstore or elsewhere. This clicker is tied to their BlackBoard ID and can be used in any couse where TurningPoint is used.
  • Instructors that would like to make TurningPoint Clickers a required course material for their courses must click "Assign TurningPoint Clickers" in the "Books/Supplies Main Menu" in Banner. This will inform students that TurningPoint clickers are required for the course and will need to be purchased at the WCC Bookstore or elsewhere. A TurningPoint receiver will be provided to faculty from the Media Services department on a semester loan basis.
  • Instructors are no longer required to attend the Media Services TurningPoint workshops to receive TurningPoint Clickers. Training workshops are still offered throughout the year and Instructors are highly encouraged to attend. For more information on training workshops click here.
  • Students are now required to register their clickers in BlackBoard. It is advisable that Instructors remind their students to complete this step prior to the first day of class.

The TurningPoint software is free. The latest version can be downloaded here (Windows users) and here (Mac users).

Important information for Mac users: currently TurningPoint will only run in conjunction with Office 2004. There are also limitations to the functionality with the Mac side version of the software. At this time WCC offers limited support for TurningPoint for Mac.

If you require assistance with installing the software, contact the WCC Help Desk and schedule an installation date.
