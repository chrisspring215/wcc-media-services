Advanced Learning Technologies

In addition to supporting Classroom and Event technologies on WCC's campus, Media Services also supports and promotes new and emerging learning technologies. These technologies help to provide innovative learning enviroments in the classroom and can provide new ways for Instrudtors to present content to students.

While some of the Advanced Learning Technologies, such as TurningPoint Clickers and the Lecture Capture systems are available for use, other technology programs are still in development and are not yet available for wide-spread use.

Advanced Learning Technology can be requested by contacting the Media Services Department directly at 734-973-3382.

TurningPoint Changes

As of the Winter 2012 Semester, WCC has shifted to a student-ownership model for TurningPoint Clickers. The new model simplifies the process for acquiring TurningPoint hardware and creating student Participant Lists.
