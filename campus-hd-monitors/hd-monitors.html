Campus HD Monitors

Media Services maintains approximately 16 HD monitors across WCC for campus use. The system is designed to provide importiant information to students, staff, faculty and guests.

If there is a malfunctioning monitor please call: 734-677-5180.

Please note: Media Services is responsible for providing technical support for the HD monitor system. We do not provide or approve content for the system.

For information on posting content to the Campus HD monitors please contact Public Relations/Marketing at 734-973-3704.
