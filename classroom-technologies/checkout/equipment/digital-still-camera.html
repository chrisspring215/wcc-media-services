Digital Still Camera

Overview

Digital Still Cameras can be used to take still photos of an event, object, or lecture. Photos can be edited using digital photo editing software to create interesting and engaging content.

Digital still cameras capture images to SD Cards that can be easily uploaded a computer for storage or editing.

Features

  • Take high quality images
  • Large storage space for bigger projects
