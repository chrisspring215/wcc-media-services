Personal Checkout Technology List

Below is a complete list of all of Media Services technology available for staff and faculty checkout. To checkout technology please visit our online checkout form. Checkouts can also be made in person at the Media Services Department Customer Counter located in the 2nd floor of the GM building, room GM-223.

Checkouts are limited to a 5-day period and are only available to staff and faculty. Please remember to review our checkout policies.

If you require training, please visit the training section of this site to view videos on our most popular technology. If you need instruction on how to operate of our technology, contact us directly at 734-677-5180.
