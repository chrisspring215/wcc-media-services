Classroom Technologies

The Media Services Department provides a variety of classroom technologies to staff and faculty to enhance classroom instruction.

Classroom technologies such as multimedia projectors, wireless microphones, and document cameras can be delivered directly to your classroom and set-up by our helpful staff.

We also provide a variety of options to staff and faculty for Personal Checkout including laptop computers, digital still cameras, digital video cameras, and multimedia projectors.

Our staff can provide training for any of our classroom technologies. Feel free to schedule a training session or take a look at our helpful training videos.

TurningPoint Clickers and Lecture Review Systems are not available through standard checkout or delivery procedures. Please visit the Advanced Learning Technologies page to learn more about how to use these technologies in your WCC classroom.
