Classroom Delivery Policies

We ask that you request classroom setup online at least two days in advance. Please call Media Services for "last minute" requests.

As of November 8, 2010, Media Services does not support Blu-Ray DVD technology on a campus-wide basis at WCC. This is due to infrastructure and equipment concerns. The only exception to this policy is LA 175 (a.k.a. "the College Theater"), which has a Blu-Ray player and a High-Definition (HD) projector installed in the room. Media Services may offer limited support for Blu-Ray DVD technology in the coming years as infrastructure and equipment issues are addressed.

Need Equipment Delivered?

Media Services can deliver, set-up, and walk you through much of our Classroom Technology.
