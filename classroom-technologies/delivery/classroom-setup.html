Classroom Delivery

Media Services provides the delivery of Classroom Technologies to classrooms on the WCC campus.

Our mission is to deliver, setup, and troubleshoot the technologies that instructors use to enhance delivery of instructional content.

To request a Classroom Technology delivery, please use one of the following methods.

  • Online Delivery Request
  • Phone: 734-973-3382
  • In person: GM-223
  • Email

A complete inventory of Classroom Technology available for delivery can be viewed here.

Please allow two days of prior notice when requesting a delivery. Emergency requests can be made by calling the Media Services directly at 734-973-3382. To schedule an reoccurring setup (standing order), please contact us directly at 734-477-8554.

Please review our policies for more information.

Technology Emergencies?

Our technicians are ready to help. If you are experiencing equipment emergencies please call: 734-677-5180.
