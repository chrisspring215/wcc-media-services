Classroom Delivery Technology List

Below is a complete list of all of Media Services technology available to be delivered directly to your classroom. To order technology please visit the online Classroom Technology Delivery form or contact us directly at 734-973-3382.

Please allow 2 days of prior notice when scheduling a Classroom Delivery.

If you require training, please visit the training section of this site to view videos on our most popular technologies. If you need instruction on how to operate our technologies, contact us directly at 734-973-3382.

Need Training?

Media Services can walk you through how to operate any technology we provide. Give us a call a call at 734-973-3382.
