Multimedia Projector Cart

Overview

The multi-media projector cart is a extremely mobile solution for presenting video, PowerPoint presentations, and web content. Instructors have the option to display media from a laptop computer, DVD or VHS playback deck. The cart is also equipped with a powered speaker for audio playback.

Projector carts are not equipped with computers. If you require a computer for your presentation, please consider the combo cart.

Features

  • VGA port for laptop projection
  • Plays and projects DVD and VHS media
  • Powered speaker for audio playback
  • Push button switcher for quick and easy switching between sources
