Easel with Flipchart Paper

Overview

Flipchart easels are a portable and simple way to communicate and collaborate with your audience. They are ideal for breakout discussions and communicating with small groups.

Features

  • Offers a low-tech solution for sharing information
  • Very portable and perfect for small group discussion
