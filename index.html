WCC Media Services Home

The Media Services Department provides Classroom Technologies, Live-Event Services, Media Production, and Media Duplication and Transfer Services.

Media Services also provides Advanced Learning Technologies such as TurningPoint Clickers and Lecture Review Systems.

The department collaborates with several campus departments including:

  • Information Technology Services and Facilities regarding classroom design standards, renovation projects and new construction
  • Conference Services regarding live-event production
  • Public Relations and Marketing regarding the Campus HD Monitors
