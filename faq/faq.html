F.A.Q.

Below are common questions that can help guild you to the information you are looking for. If you are still unable to find the correct information please call Rick Cooco at 734-477-8554.

  • How do I get audio-visual equipment to use in my classroom?
  • Services can be requested by calling Media Services at 734-677-5180.
  • Where is Media Services located?
  • Media Services offices are located in GM 223 on second floor of the Gunder Myran Building.
  • How do I learn to use a multimedia (both video and data) projector?
  • Faculty and staff should call Rick Cocco in Media Services at 734-477-8554.
  • Who can coordinate audio/visual in Towsley Auditorium?
  • Call Warren Nelson in Media Services at 734-477-8558.
  • Who can coordinate lighting in Towsley Auditorium?
  • Call Warren Nelson in Media Services at 734-477-8558.

Technology Emergencies?

Our technicians are ready to help. If you are experiencing technology emergencies please call 734-677-5180.
