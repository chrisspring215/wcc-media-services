Media Duplication and Transfer

Media Duplication: We can duplicate up to 50 DVDs or CDs for college uses within the constraints of applicable copyright laws. Quantities of more than 50 are also possible via budget transfer. Please contact us at 734-973-3382 if your project requires more than 50 copies.

Media Format Transfer: We can convert between various video, computer, and audio formats such as DVD, CD, cassette, VHS, mini-DV, MPEG-4, etc. for college uses within the constraints of applicable copyright laws.

Important: Please review our policies page regarding Media Duplication and Transfer services.
